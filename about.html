About the TSE Academy

An initiative to equip teammates with the skills to become Technical Support Engineers

Our Initiative

The NORAM TSE Academy is an initiative designed to equip teammates with the skills, knowledge, and resources they need to become successful Technical Support Engineers. We provide a structured path for growth and development in the technical support field.

What We Offer

Our academy offers targeted training, mentorship opportunities, and hands-on experience to help teammates develop both technical expertise and customer service skills essential for the TSE role.

Core Values

  • Empowerment: Giving teammates the tools they need to succeed
  • Collaboration: Learning together through knowledge sharing
  • Growth: Continuous improvement and skill development
